The ceremony and reception will both take place at the Nonantum Resort, Kennebunkport ME
4:00PM: Ceremony on the lawn (weather permitting)

6:00PM: Dinner and reception
10:30PM: Reception ends. After-party at Federal Jacks Restaurant and Brew Pub (8 Western Ave, Kennebunk, ME). Town trolley shuttle will be available to take guests between the Nonantum Resort and Federal Jacks throughout the night until the bar closes at 1:00AM
Note: Please make sure to bring a valid US ID/passport as it is required to enter the bar due to very strict drinking rules in Maine!

Saturday June 6th, 2015
For those staying in Kennebunkport, brunch will be served at the Nonantum Resort, Kennebunkport ME, from 9-11am.
